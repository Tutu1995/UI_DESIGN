Intelligent Wardrobe

Designed by Jing Tu

Topic Description

Q: Why make an app about this?

A: Dealing with excessive amount of items in peopleâ€™s wardrobes and selecting an outfit for daily life on different purpose can be a tedious and messy process.

For example, sometimes, people who put attention on the appearance of their clothing and pursuit fashion match may have difficulties in wardrobe management and dressing decisions; meanwhile, sometimes based on activity situation like business, casual , sport day , or based on weather situation like sunny, rainy or snow day, people will care more about the function and utility of clothes.

Thereby, In order to help these individuals, we can develop an app called Intelligent Wardrobe to help them select clothes without effort in a timely manner.

Potential Apps

* refs to outside resources, references, or data sets related to my topic.

  1. Smart Mirror (*article) (*video)
    • connect your mirror with wardrobe so that mirror has saved info of inventory of items in an individual's wardrobe.
    • mirror display information on-screen about weather, daily plans, time, and automatically give user outfit options.
    • through mirror, user can see combinations of clothing without physically putting them on.
    • user also can interact with the mirror and control the combinations of clothing by gesture swapping in front of mirror.
  2. Fashion Helper (*article)
    • connect your phone with your wardrobe to provides clothing inventory mobile view.
    • app allows the person to see and choose different permutations of attire wherever and whenever they are.
    • user can apply their own selfies and body size to a virtual model.
    • connect with other online shopping apps if user cannot find any satisfactory clothes.
    • allow user to sold, exchange or donate their clothing online.
