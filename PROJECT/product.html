Overview

PROBLEM

Dealing with excessive amount of items in peopleâ€™s wardrobes and selecting an outfit for daily life on different purpose can be a tedious and messy process. For example, people who put attention on the appearance of their clothing and pursuit fashion match may have difficulties in dressing decisions; And, based on activity situations like business, casual, sport day, or based on weather situation like sunny or rainy day, people will care more about the function and utility of clothes. Thereby, In order to help these individuals, we can develop an app called Intelligent Wardrobe to help them select clothes without effort in a timely manner.

SOLUTION

I decided to tackle this problem by designing a mobile application for people. The main features include the ability to display information on-screen about weather, daily plans, time, and automatically give user outfit options. so user can see combinations of clothing without physically putting them on. The app provides people with portable access to the contents of their closet so they can check their closet inventory before making a purchase and donating/trading unwanted clothes. Also, user can set location-enabled tags called RFID tags on clothing items to help with inventory tracking.

Features

Visualize Outfits

See your outfits on screen virtually instead of putting any clothes on, in order to help user with decision-making.

Scan And Label Items

The app can scan and save clothing items info, also allow to label items based on your prefences like weather, utility and activity.

Locate Items

With RFID labels attached to your clothing, quickly locate item in your closet.

Manage Inventory

manage your inventory of clothes to making a purchase or donating/trading unwanted clothes.
